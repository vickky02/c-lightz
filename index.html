Welcome to LIGHTZ

Lightz technology deploys an innovative advertising display system, which illuminates sponsor logos and real-time race information within the wheel hubs of some of the world's fastest cars.

Images can be pre-programmed or updated remotely to display different information at different points during the race. The illumination is bright, static and remains upright regardless of speed.

The result is highly visible to both trackside and TV audiences. Motorsport media is both targeted and measurable and is ideal for product launches and brand awareness campaigns.

The technology has been developed in conjunction with Formula 1 and IndyCar teams. The system is triggered automatically when the car is in motion.
