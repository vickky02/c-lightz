How it works

Brand owners, Series and Teams will send their required image file to the Speed of Light server which checks, converts and sends each file on to the relevant control computer, with all race schedule information and logos. The individual wheel units are also updated via the Internet prior to each race.

KEY FEATURES

  • Flexible – Brands can show different logo(s) and messages during any/each race.
  • Relevant – Brands can decide the precise content for each race or even just for a segment of a race.
  • Time specific – Race information within the wheels draws the eyes of the viewers to the wheel display and thus any accompanying advertising.
  • Self-contained – System is self-contained within each car, and sensors can individually adjust the rate that the content is displayed.
  • Connected – Technology is fully inter-active and updated in real-time.
  • Fully monitored – All units are constantly monitored during each race.
  • Innovative – New motorsport sponsors may well be attracted to this novel technology
